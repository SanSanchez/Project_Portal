Project Portal

Student Company

Need a Project Done?

Project Portal is a web-based platform for Computer Science, Programming, Software Engineering or Information Technology College Students seeking class room projects using “real-world” programming projects from local business and companies.

Our interactive approach focuses on finding innovative students searching for “classroom” projects from local business and companies. Students work in teams or individually for the duration of a semester to provide project solutions. In exchange, students will gain programming experience, boost their resumes, and/or receive a grade recommendation for the project or possibly secure a job offer.

Companies will get projects done and find future entry level talents by accessing the student’s classroom problem solving methods. This will save time and money.
